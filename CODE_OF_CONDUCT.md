# Code of Conduct

## 1. 协作期待 / Collaboration Expectations

中文：
欢迎具体、直接、尊重的交流。请把讨论重点放在问题、证据和改进方案上。

English:
Specific, direct, and respectful collaboration is welcome. Keep the discussion focused on problems, evidence, and proposed improvements.

## 2. 不可接受的行为 / Unacceptable Behavior

中文：
- 人身攻击
- 骚扰或歧视
- 恶意羞辱
- 故意破坏讨论环境

English:
- Personal attacks
- Harassment or discrimination
- Malicious humiliation
- Intentional disruption of the collaboration environment
